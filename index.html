Professional Background

Barrister Saad Andalib Aristu was called to the Bar at Lincoln's Inn, UK. Upon returning to Bangladesh, he established a distinguished practice at the Supreme Court, specializing in corporate and human rights law.

His approach combines traditional legal excellence with modern technology, ensuring clients receive efficient, strategic, and results-driven representation. He is regularly consulted by major corporations and international NGOs.

Committed to access to justice, Barrister Saad also provides pro bono services to underprivileged communities and actively contributes to legal education as a visiting faculty member.
